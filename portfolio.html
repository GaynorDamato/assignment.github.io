About

Hi! I'm a Bachelor of Fine Arts in Digital Arts students at the University of Malta, graduating in 2020. This Portfolio website allows me to share my projects with the public. These works showcase some of the projects throughout my three-year course at the University, which feature different kinds of projects, from UX design to brand identities and illustrations.
