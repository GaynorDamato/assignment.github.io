Miro

Miro held his empty glass between his frail hands as he observed young families passing by. His white hair drifted slowly in the wind as his head faced the pavement and his big square glasses barely held on his nose. Sitting on his favourite blue stool in front of his house, with his elbows resting on his knees, his thoughts drifted. Miro was amused by the rainbow after the rain that, for him, never seemed to shine when he stepped out his door, and by his only companion, Ben. The white and grey cat was like a son to him. Miro spent hours talking to his cat which he rescued from his own street. Overall, his dream was to be welcomed with warmth and joy by his own family that never visited him, and to wake up every day to an everlasting bright rainbow which he was mesmerised by. Unfortunately, his time on this earth was limited and he knew that his wishes would never be met.
